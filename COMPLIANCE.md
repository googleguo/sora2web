# VideoForge AI - Creem Payment Compliance

This document outlines how VideoForge AI complies with Creem's Merchant of Record requirements for AI wrapper products.

## 1. Product Branding and Naming ✅

**Requirement:** Develop independent branding without using AI model brand names in product name.

**Implementation:**
- Product name: **VideoForge AI**
- No reference to underlying AI model providers in branding
- Clear, unique identity separate from AI models used
- SEO domains can be used with redirects if needed

## 2. Marketing and Transparency ✅

**Disclaimers:**
- Footer includes disclaimer: "VideoForge AI is an independent platform and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by any AI model providers. We provide custom interfaces and features for third-party AI models."
- Pricing page includes disclaimer about trademark compliance
- FAQ clearly states independent nature

**Feature Representation:**
- Only advertise features actually delivered
- Clear about capabilities and limitations
- Transparent pricing and credit system

## 3. Platform and Product Integrity ✅

**Implementation:**
- Single, clearly defined product
- Clear disclosure of wrapper nature throughout site
- Transparent about being custom interface for AI models
- Example disclosure in FAQ: "VideoForge AI is an independent AI video generation platform that creates high-quality videos from text prompts or images. We provide a custom interface built on top of advanced AI models to enhance usability and provide additional features. We are not affiliated with the underlying AI model providers."

## 4. Customer Experience and Support ✅

**Support Channels:**
- Visible support email: **support@videoforge.ai**
- Email prominently displayed in:
  - Footer of every page
  - Support page (/support)
  - Pricing page
  - FAQ sections

**Response Time:**
- Committed to 3 business day response time
- Clearly communicated on support page

**Subscription Management:**
- Users can cancel subscriptions from account settings
- Integration with Creem Customer Portal
- Clear cancellation process documented

## 5. Refund Policy ✅

**Implementation:**
- 7-day money-back guarantee for first-time subscribers
- Clearly stated in pricing FAQ
- Contact support@videoforge.ai for refunds
- Committed to responding within 3 business days
